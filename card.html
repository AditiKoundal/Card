Crayon Shin-chan is a popular Japanese manga and anime series created by Yoshito Usui in 1990, featuring a mischievous, 5-year-old boy named Shinnosuke "Shin-chan" Nohara. Based in Kasukabe, Japan, the show follows his chaotic adventures with family, friends, and pet dog Shiro, known for his unique humor and adult-like demeanor. The show has been running since 1992 and is a global hit.

Doremon

Doraemon is a famous Japanese manga and anime series created by Fujiko F. Fujio in 1969, featuring a robotic cat from the 22nd century who travels back in time to help a young boy named Nobita Nobi using futuristic gadgets. Known for its themes of friendship and imagination, it is a beloved global icon with numerous anime adaptations, films, and merchandise.
